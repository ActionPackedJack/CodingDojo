Some things everyone should know about ninja:

  • They performed acts of espionage, guerilla warfare, sabotage, and sometimes assassination.
  • They were most active during the 16th century... as far as we know.
  • Contrary to the Samurai code of honor, they considered no tactic off-limits in matters of life or death.
  • The most famous ninja was Hattori Hanzo, who played an instrumental role in Ieyasu Tokugawa's unification of Japan.
